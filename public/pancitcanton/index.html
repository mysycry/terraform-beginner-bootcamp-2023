About Us


Tradition since 2020

Welcome to "Noodle Delight," your gateway to the savory world of Pancit Canton. Our restaurant offers a cozy and vibrant setting where the aroma of sizzling noodles and fresh ingredients fills the air. Indulge in our authentic Pancit Canton dishes, prepared with the finest ingredients and served with a smile, making every visit a delightful culinary experience.

Our Menu


Noodle Stir-fry

It’s so popular that you can find it everywhere, literally – from street vendors, to wet markets, food stalls, office or school cafeteria, mall food courts – it’s everywhere. It is everyone’s favorite noodles, indeed!


Seafood Pancit Canton

Dive into the flavors of the sea with succulent shrimp, tender squid, and mussels, combined with aromatic garlic and ginger, creating a seafood lover's dream.


Spicy Pancit Canton

Ignite your taste buds with a fiery twist on the classic, as red chili flakes and chili sauce add a kick to the stir-fried noodles, balancing heat with the freshness of vegetables.


Vegetarian Pancit Canton

A meatless delight filled with an array of crisp, garden-fresh veggies, tofu, and a flavorful vegetarian oyster sauce, providing a satisfying and wholesome option for all.
